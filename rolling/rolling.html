What is Clean Architecture?

Clean Architecture promotes the idea of modular, testable, and maintainable code. It emphasizes separating concerns and defining clear boundaries between system components.

What is Platform Engineering?

Platform engineering brings self-service capabilities and automated infrastructure operations to improve the developer experience and reduce complexity.
